REFUGEE LEARNING ACCELERATOR

We support engineers and computer scientists from the Middle East to
create educational technologies for refugee learners

The Refugee Learning Accelerator supports engineers and computer scientists from the Middle East to create technologies for refugee learners. We provide access to learning, mentorship, and funding. We are not a typical accelerator: we're more interested in supporting a community of innovators to learn and explore together, than in launching the next billion dollar company.

The Accelerator is a project of the Media Lab Learning Initiative. The Media Lab is an antidisciplinary research lab at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology that designs technologies for people to create a better future.
